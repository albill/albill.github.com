Church Interior I've always been fascinated by Swedenborg, who went from being a scientist to becoming a visionary. Swedenborg's impact on the Western Esoteric tradition was quite high and elements of his thought can still be found today in various groups and traditions. I had heard, when I lived in Seattle, that there was some kind of Swedenborgian church in another city nearby but few details. It isn't my cup of tea but I'm glad they are out there.
