--- wordpress_id: 1479 layout: post title: Fudo Myoo date: 2006-12-14 00:43:11 wordpress_url: http://www.arcanology.com/?p=1479 --- Fudo Myoo is the patron of both Tendai and Shingon Buddhism, the Japanese forms of Vajrayana. He is the immovable one and the wrathful form of Dainichi Nyorai, who embodies the Dharmakaya.
