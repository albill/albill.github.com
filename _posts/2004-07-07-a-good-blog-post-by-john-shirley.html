"A meaning at the core of life, a sense of connection--we don't have it. We think sometimes that people in “Shangri-La” will have it, the lamas of The Razor's Edge, the American Indian, the Japanese. So we fantasize that we go in and get that meaning--we get it from them just the way we get land by moving in on it and gold by digging for it and oil by drilling for it!"
