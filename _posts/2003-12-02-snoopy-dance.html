The new (and only modern) translation of Iamblichus' On the Mysteries just showed up. This was done by John Dillon, Emma Clarke and others. The only translation previously available in this century was from the turn of the century (the Wilde translation) and the next one from 100 years before that. Both were less than ideal. This edition even has the critical edition of the Greek text on the facing pages. WOW!!!
